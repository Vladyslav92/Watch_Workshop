The company

Dmitry Kotov's watch studio has been operating in Kyiv since 2011.

We produce steel watches under the D.Kotov brand. The production

base is locatedon the territory of the Itkol Technopark on 1st Frezernaya Street.

The workshop is equipped with modern metalworking equipment

with numerical control, which allows for milling, turning,

electrical erosion and laser processing, electrolyte-plasma polishing

of stainless steel and other technological operations. Our watches use

Ukrainian and Japanese calibers.
